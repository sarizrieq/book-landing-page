About the Book

This book serves as a concise and practical guide, specifically designed to help you with your MATAM exam. It offers clear, easily digestible explanations and well-structured examples that break down complex concepts, making them easy to understand and apply.

This book simplifies complex topics and provides a clear understanding of design patterns. Tailored for students, it breaks down intricate concepts in a straightforward manner, making even the most challenging subjects easy to grasp and apply.
