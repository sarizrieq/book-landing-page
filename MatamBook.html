About the Book

This book serves as a concise and practical guide, specifically designed to help students with their MATAM exam. It simplifies complex topics and provides a clear understanding of design patterns. Tailored for students, it breaks down intricate concepts in a straightforward manner, making even the most challenging subjects easy to grasp and apply.
